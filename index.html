About Me

A year ago I realized that something was missing from my life. I loved my job (and still do), there was no doubt that, but I also began to feel like I needed more; a new goal perhaps that could satisfy my curiosity, my creativity. That's when I've started to learn front-end development, and with that I've finally found my happy place.
Imagine the joy I've felt every time I sat down in front of my computer and thought about what to create next! I had no trouble learning and developing my skills on my own, as I've been a self-motivated and independent person all my life. So far I've learnt how to use HTML 5, CSS, JavaScript, and how to work on responsive design. My personal favourite is mminimalistic design, due to its clean and elegant features. But I'm not planning to stop here:
I'm always looking for the latest design elements, because I want my knowledge to be up to date, and my clients to have the coolest websites out there.

Can't wait to work with you!
